The benefits of what Fiber Optics can do for internet and what IdeaTek has to offer.

Fiber optics transmit data as light pulses through thin strands of glass or plastic, with the core, cladding, and buffer coating ensuring efficient transmission. To maximize fiber internet speeds and internal network performance, ensure professional installation with minimal cable bends, use high-quality and up-to-date equipment, and prefer wired Ethernet connections over wireless. Position your router centrally and elevated, manage network settings for device prioritization, and minimize interference from other electronic devices. Additionally, use gigabit Ethernet cables, and ensure all connected devices and networking hardware are capable of handling high-speed connections. Implementing these practices will help you achieve the highest possible speeds both from your fiber internet connection and within your internal network.
